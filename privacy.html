Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026
The short version: Your debt data never leaves your device. The only data ever sent anywhere is AI chat messages (Pro feature only), which go to our secure server and are never stored.

1. Who We Are

ZERO is a debt payoff calculator app. For questions, contact us at privacy@zerodebt.app.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal information. Specifically:

  • Your debt data (names, balances, interest rates) is stored only on your device using the browser's localStorage. It is never transmitted to any server.
  • The app has no account system, no login, and no user profiles.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any identifying information.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs.

3. AI Advisor (Pro Feature)

If you use the AI Advisor (available with ZERO Pro):

  • Your chat messages and a summary of your debt profile are sent to our secure server to generate a response.
  • These messages are not stored on our servers. They are passed through to the AI provider (Google Gemini) and discarded.
  • Your full debt data is never sent — only the information needed to answer your specific question.
  • If you use your own API key (BYOK), your messages go directly through our proxy to Google's API.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API — used to power the AI Advisor. Google's privacy policy applies to AI chat messages: policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — used to process in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — standard app store purchase processing.

5. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). We receive confirmation of your subscription status but not your payment details. Subscription management is handled through your device's App Store settings.

6. Children's Privacy

ZERO is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at privacy@zerodebt.app.
