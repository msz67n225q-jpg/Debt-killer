FREE TIPS
1
Pay on the due date, not the statement date
Interest accrues daily. Paying right on the due date (not early) is fine — but never after.
2
Round every payment up to the nearest $50
Even $25 extra per month can shave months off your payoff and save hundreds in interest.
3
Set up autopay for the minimum
One missed payment can trigger a penalty APR. Autopay prevents that — then pay extra manually on top.
4
Pay high-rate cards twice a month
Splitting your monthly payment in two reduces your average daily balance — and the interest you owe.
5
Put every windfall straight at your debt
Tax refunds, bonuses, side income — a single lump-sum payment hits principal directly and compounds forward.
